Some interesting facts about the new Audi A8

 
	
Editorial office of Motor.kz   03.01.2022


Very soon, the updated fourth generation (D5) of the luxury German Audi A8 car will be on sale, which premiered back in November 2021.


Against this background, we decided to tell you some interesting facts about this car and what is new in it.


And so, in addition to the fact that the car has changed a little externally, it has been equipped with new headlights, each of which contains 1.3 million micro mirrors. These mirrors are able not only to direct the luminous flux in the right direction, but also to generate animation, as well as to project various pictograms on the road surface.



Since the Audi A8 has always been a car for passengers, the main changes in the interior were made for them. Now the passengers in the back row have a large enough refrigerator designed for two bottles, for example champagne, and so that they don't get bored on the road, they can enjoy watching their favorite movie, for which a 10.1-inch screen is installed in front of each passenger.



Also, passengers can control not only the settings of the climate system, but also the multimedia system, independently choosing the content that they like, and not the driver. Moreover, a special footrest with massage function is provided for all passengers, including the passenger in the front row. How do you like that?



As for the engines, nothing has changed in this regard. The updated version of the Audi A8 car will be equipped with the same engines, including:


  • Petrol 3.0-liter V6 with 340 horsepower
  • Diesel 3.0-liter V6 with 286 horsepower
  • Petrol 4.0-liter V8 with 460 horsepower
  • Petrol 4.0-liter V8 with 571 horsepower
  • Hybrid powerplant PHEV with a capacity of 462 horsepower.

The claimed acceleration from 0 to 100 km / h. the 571-horsepower Audi A8 is 3.8 seconds, which is very good for a car of this class.


Regardless of the configuration, all cars will be equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission (automatic transmission) and all-wheel drive.


In Germany, the cost of this car will start from 99.5 thousand euros or about 48 million Kazakhstani tenge at the current exchange rate. In general, it's not cheap.
